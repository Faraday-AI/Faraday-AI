See changes as they happen, get notified when colleagues join or leave sessions, and collaborate seamlessly without conflicts. Your AI assistant tracks collaboration activity, manages session participants, and provides analytics on collaboration metrics—all accessible through natural conversation.

Team Coordination & Activity Collaboration: Coordinate with your teaching team, plan activities together, and share resources effortlessly. The collaboration system supports activity-specific collaboration, allowing PE teachers to collaborate on activity plans, science teachers to work together on lab designs, or administrators to coordinate school-wide initiatives. Your avatar helps manage team members, track collaboration progress, and ensure everyone stays informed. "Create a collaboration team for the math department" or "Add the science teachers to the lab planning session"—your assistant makes team coordination as simple as having a conversation.
