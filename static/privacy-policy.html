Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Faraday AI, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational technology platform and SMS messaging services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, school affiliation, and role (teacher, student, parent, administrator)
  • SMS Subscription Information: Phone number, consent records, message preferences, and opt-in/opt-out status
  • Educational Data: Student records, grades, assignments, attendance, and other educational information
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and other communications you send through our platform

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Location data (if permitted)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our educational technology services
  • Send SMS messages for educational purposes, including account alerts, educational updates, and school notifications
  • Process transactions and manage accounts
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account, services, and updates
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. SMS Messaging

When you opt-in to receive SMS messages, you consent to receive text messages from Faraday AI, LLC for educational purposes. These messages may include:

  • Account alerts and notifications
  • Educational updates and resources from your child's teacher
  • Important school and classroom notifications
  • Service notifications related to your child's education

Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Your consent to receive SMS messages is separate from your agreement to our Terms of Service.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Schools and Educational Institutions: To provide educational services and facilitate communication between teachers, students, and parents
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., SMS delivery, cloud hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for educational use and may collect information from students under 13. We comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Student data is collected and used solely for educational purposes and is not used for commercial purposes.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal and contractual obligations)
  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Educational records are retained in accordance with applicable education laws and regulations.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
