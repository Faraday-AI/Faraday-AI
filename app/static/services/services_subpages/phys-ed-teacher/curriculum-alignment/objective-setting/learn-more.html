What is Objective Setting?

Objective setting in physical education involves defining clear, measurable learning goals that guide your teaching and help students understand what they are expected to achieve. Well-defined objectives provide direction for both instruction and assessment.

How It Works

  1. Identify the key skills and knowledge to be learned
  2. Write clear, measurable objectives using action verbs
  3. Align objectives with curriculum standards
  4. Develop assessment criteria
  5. Plan instruction to meet the objectives
